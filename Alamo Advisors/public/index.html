Fiduciary Guidance

We operate under a fiduciary standard, meaning we are required to act in your best interest and put your needs ahead of our own.

Our Approach: We manage accounts on a non-discretionary basis, ensuring you retain complete control and transparency. You pre-approve each investment transaction we recommend, combining professional expertise with your ultimate decision-making authority. We primarily recommend stocks, bonds, and mutual funds to construct sophisticated, tax-efficient portfolios.

Account Minimums: We require a $5 million minimum account size, though this may be negotiable under certain circumstances. We may group certain related client accounts for the purpose of achieving the minimum account size.

Investment advisory services involve risk. Please review our Form CRS and Form ADV for important disclosures about our services, fees, conflicts of interest, and brokerage practices.

Why Choose Meridian Advisors

What sets us apart

True Independence & Objectivity

As a completely independent firm, we maintain zero affiliations with broker-dealers, insurance companies, or investment product vendors. Our recommendations are based exclusively on your best interest—never influenced by commissions, sales quotas, or proprietary products.

Transparent Fee Structure

We charge a transparent quarterly fee based on assets under management. Our investment advisory fees are separate from any optional tax preparation or accounting services. No commissions on investment products. Our compensation rises only when your portfolio grows—complete alignment with your financial success.

SEC Registered Fiduciary

We are registered with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission and legally bound to the highest fiduciary standard. Unlike broker-dealers held to a lower "suitability" standard, we are required by law to act in your best interest at all times.

Sophisticated Investment Approach

Our investment focus includes corporate debt, mutual funds, municipal securities, and for qualified investors: real estate partnerships, oil and gas partnerships, and private equity arrangements. We provide institutional-quality portfolio construction typically reserved for endowments and pension funds. Client accounts are custodied primarily at RBC Capital Markets.
